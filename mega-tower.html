The Day I Built a Mega Tower

Mar 2026 • Evan’s Adventure World

One morning, I decided that I wanted to build the tallest block tower I had ever made. I brought all of my blocks to the middle of the room and made a big colorful pile.

First, I made a strong base with the biggest blocks so the tower wouldn’t fall over. Then I started stacking smaller blocks on top, one by one, as carefully as I could. I had to stand up on my toes to reach the top!

The tower grew higher and higher. It was almost as tall as the table lamp. I felt proud and a little bit nervous every time I added a new block.

Finally, I said, “Just one more block!” I reached up, put it on the top… and the whole tower started to wobble. For a second it stayed up, and then… crash! Blocks went everywhere.

I laughed, because even though it fell down, building it was still really fun. Now I know that if I want to go higher next time, I have to make the bottom even stronger.

Maybe my next adventure will be building a tower that doesn’t fall at all— or maybe an even bigger one that makes an even bigger crash.
