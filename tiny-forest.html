Exploring the Tiny Forest

Mar 2026 • Evan’s Adventure World

Near my house, there is a small group of trees. To grown‑ups it looks like just a little patch of green, but to me, it is a whole forest.

One day, I decided to explore it like a real explorer. I picked up a stick to be my walking stick, put on my favorite shoes, and stepped into the “tiny forest”.

I walked on crunchy leaves and tiny paths between the trees. I saw ants carrying food, heard birds singing above me, and found a leaf that looked like a little green boat.

I pretended that the trees were giant towers and that I was on a secret mission. Every new stick, rock, and leaf was a clue that I had to look at very carefully.

When it was time to go home, I said goodbye to the tiny forest and promised to come back. Even though it is small, it feels like a whole adventure world just for me.
