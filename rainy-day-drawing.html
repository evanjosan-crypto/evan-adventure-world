Rainy Day Drawing Party

Mar 2026 • Evan’s Adventure World

One day, it rained from morning all the way until bedtime. I couldn’t go outside to play, so I decided to have a drawing party instead.

I spread paper all over the table and took out every crayon I own. There were reds, blues, greens, and even a sparkly gold one that I like to save for special drawings.

First, I drew a rocket ship flying through space with stars and planets. Then I drew a dragon that looked a little bit silly, but I liked it anyway. After that, I drew a big rainbow that almost didn’t fit on the page.

While the rain tapped on the windows, I kept drawing and coloring. The table turned into a gallery full of my pictures. It felt like the whole room was brighter, even though it was gray outside.

By the end of the day, my hands were covered in crayon colors and I was tired but happy. A rainy day turned into a colorful adventure right at the kitchen table.
